Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1st July, 2025

This Chrome extension, ChatGPT TOC Extension, respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is (and is not) collected and how it is used.

Information Collection and Use

  • This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing activity.
  • No information is sent to any external servers or third parties.
  • All processing is done locally in your browser.

Permissions

The extension may request permissions necessary for its functionality (such as reading the content of the current page to generate a table of contents), but it does not use these permissions to collect or transmit data.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the author via the GitHub repository.
